Content Permission Check List

This page is part of the Modding Helper documentation.

This page lists the currently supported upload restrictions, conditional permissions, creator guidelines, and past DMCA notices used by Modding Helper's “Content Permission Check”.

Notes
This list is intended to help verify whether content may require caution before use or upload.
Warnings are based on osu!official permissions, public creator guidelines, and past DMCA notices, but do not guarantee the legal status of any content.

Entries such as HOOKSOFT or KeroQ / Makura that contain multiple song titles use representative examples only and are not exhaustive lists.

When available, official guideline pages and original DMCA notice texts are linked directly.
